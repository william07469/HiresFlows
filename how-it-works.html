What is ATS and why does it matter?
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software companies use to filter CVs before human eyes see them. 75% of CVs are rejected by these systems. Our tool optimizes your CV to pass through.
How accurate is the ATS score?
Our ATS score is based on keyword matching, format analysis, and readability factors. It's a reliable indicator of how well your CV will perform, but actual results vary by company and industry.

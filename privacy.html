Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

CV/Resume Content: When you upload or paste your CV, we process it to provide our services. This content is sent to Google's Gemini AI API for rewriting and analysis.

Job Descriptions: When you provide a job description for keyword matching, we process it alongside your CV.

Payment Information: Payments are processed by Whop. We do not store your credit card details. We receive payment confirmation and plan status from Whop.

Usage Data: We track the number of CV fixes, feature usage, and session identifiers to manage your credits and service access.

2. How We Use Your Information

• To analyze and rewrite your CV using AI
• To generate cover letters and interview preparation materials
• To match keywords between your CV and job descriptions
• To manage your credits and subscription access
• To process payments through our payment provider

3. Data Sharing

We share your data with the following third parties:

Google (Gemini AI): Your CV content and job descriptions are sent to Google's Gemini API for AI processing. Google's privacy policy applies to this data.

Whop: Payment and subscription data is processed by Whop. Their privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention

CV content is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers. Payment and usage records are retained for the duration of your active use plus 12 months.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, rate limiting, input validation, and access controls to protect your data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Request correction of inaccurate data
• Request deletion of your data
• Object to processing of your data
• Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@hiresflows.com

7. Cookies

We use minimal browser storage (localStorage) only to maintain your session and credit count. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of significant changes through the service.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@hiresflows.com
